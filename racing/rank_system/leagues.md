Similar to many popular games, Snail Trail has 6 different Leagues to compete in. Leagues will be based on the ELO Rating System.

| League    | ELO Rating |
|-----------|------------|
| Master    | 2000+      |
| Diamond   | 1800-1999  |
| Platinum  | 1600-1799  |
| Gold      | 1400-1599  |
| Silver    | 1200-1399  |
| Bronze    | 0-1199     |
| Placement | 0          |
