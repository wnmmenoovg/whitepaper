Snail Trail uses ERC-721 NFT Standard and the following contracts to generate in-game characters.

There are 3 ways that an NFT can be generated using a Snail Trail contract.

## Incubator & Laboratory
With in-game reproduction systems owners can generate new NFTs.

## Genesis
Genesis NFTs will be generated using the Claim page before Launch. Early investors and community members can claim these NFTs at fixed prices. Genesis NFTs will be generated when an owner decides to buy them. 
There will be a maximum of 7500 Genesis NFTs. These will be available during Genesis Claim Period with a standard price.
